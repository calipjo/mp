The Patapat Viaduct is one of the most celebrated bridges in the Philippines because of its remarkable winding design. Built on the side of the Ilocos Norte mountain range, the road is approximately 1.3 kilometers in length. It has 2 lanes, one for each direction. There are some areas along the bridge where tourists can safely park their vehicle and take-in the exquisite view of Pasaleng Bay.
